# Product & Requirements

This section turns unclear business needs into engineering-ready problem statements, boundaries, requirements, and acceptance signals.

## Artifacts

- [System boundary checklist](system-boundary-checklist.md)
- [Requirements discovery techniques](requirements-discovery-techniques.md)

## Use This Section When

- A feature is described as a solution before the problem is clear.
- Multiple teams disagree about ownership or scope.
- Requirements are scattered across chats, meetings, tickets, and documents.
- Engineering work is blocked by missing acceptance criteria, external dependencies, or unclear business rules.

## Output Standard

A requirements artifact is ready when it explains the user or business outcome, names the actors, states what is in and out of scope, captures open questions, and gives engineering a testable way to say done.
